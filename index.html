At VEC, we strive to promote education and volunteering during these trying times through innovative ways. With the shift to virtual environments during this pandemic, we want to help out our community, now more than ever. From virtual lessons to raising awareness through social media, we are adapting to change in order to create change.

News

VEC is coordinating with Valley Ranch Elementary in Coppell ISD to help educate students through accessible videos tailored to their needs.

VEC is also in the process of contacting various organizations, including blood centers, to figure out ways to help them and raise awareness about the work those organizations do.
